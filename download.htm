ABSTRACT SUBMISSION DETAILS

The abstract should be written in English and sent by May 10, to egeus2021@pproevents.com
All the submitted abstracts will be published in electronic format under ISBN in a supplement of the Journal of Gastrointestinal and Liver Disease  (2.2 impact factor)

Recommended structure:
  – The text itself will have a maximum of 300 words (maximum 2000 characters, not including the title, authors and affiliation)
  – Tables, graphs and summary photos are not accepted
  – Abbreviations will be explained when they first appear in the text
  – A separate line must be inserted for a maximum of 3 keywords
  – The bibliography is not included in the text of the abstract.

IMPORTANT! You can submit an abstract either for the e-poster session or for the free communication video session that will take place on Thursday, June 2, between 16.45-17.45 (CET+1). 

IMPORTANT! For the submitted abstract to be accepted, at least the first author must be registered as a participant in the congress. The participation fee will have to be paid until May 10.


The authors of the accepted abstracts will be notified until May 14th!

VIDEO PRESENTATION – FREE COMMUNICATION

The free communication should be submitted in video format – including video and sound (.avi or .mp4 or .mpeg) 

– 16:9 format
– The video should not exceed 5 minutes and 500 MB
– Official language: English
– Case studies are recommended

The video of the free communication will be played during the dedicated session, scheduled for Thursday, June 2, between 16:45-17:45 CET+ 1 during the live session. 

The submission deadline May 10, 2022 and it should be done on the following email address egeus2021@pproevents.com 
Please use a large file transfer software and do not exceed 500MB. Thank you in advance!

E-POSTER PRESENTATION


Before submitting an e-poster, you must submit an abstract of the e-poster and receive confirmation of acceptance by May 15th and then submit your e-poster following the instructions below. Thank you for your understanding!

The e-poster presentation should be submitted in PPT format – excluding video and sound

– 16:9 PPT format, PORTRAIT
– The presentation has to be limited to only 1 slide
– Official language: English
– Case studies are recommended

E-Posters will be displayed on the event platform for the entire period of the congress and on the only platform.

The Poster Committee will grant prizes to the best presentations.

The submission deadline is May 25, 2022 and it should be done on the following email address alina.puscas@pproevents.com
